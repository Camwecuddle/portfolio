Logo design is a career path that has always intrigued me. A logo is an icon that can represent an entire company. Apple's apple for example is a world renowned logo that seems so simple yet in reality is so cleverly created. If you know what to look for, the golden ratio and Fibonacci have a huge sway on the design. I want to use my creativity to design clever and beautiful logos.
