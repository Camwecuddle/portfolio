My final project for graphic design 1 consisted of 5 steps. Cut a square straight through an entire magazine. Find a few of these squares that could tell a story. Find an excerpt from a story that can fit with the images. Interpose the story with the squares. And then glue all the squares together in an accoridon like structure. I simply chose the patterns I found that were most appealing to me. I ordered them from lightest to darkest and as you scroll through the pages you should find the theme getting slowly darker. This accents the story which is an excerpt from my favorite book, "The Name of the Wind." In the excerpt we find the main character stairing at a bright burning building and read on until just before he blacks out.
